Rules & Regulation

Housing society rules and regulations play a crucial role in creating a harmonious and well-maintained living environment for residents. These rules cover various aspects of living within the society, ensuring the safety, comfort, and well-being of all members.
1. Resident Conduct and Responsibilities:

Maintenance of property: Keeping individual flats and common areas clean and hygienic.

Noise restrictions: Avoiding excessive noise during specific times to respect neighbors' peace.

Waste disposal: Following proper waste disposal procedures to maintain cleanliness and hygiene.
2. Common Area Usage and Amenities:

Gym, swimming pool, and other facilities: Outlining booking procedures, usage timings, and dress codes.

Gardens and recreational areas: Rules for using these spaces responsibly and maintaining their upkeep.

Rooftop access: Regulations for using the rooftop for drying clothes, socializing, or installing solar panels.
